About It

This was fun to make. This video game is a Zelda-esque arcade game where you have to kill as many monsters as you can while trying to get the highest score. The further you get in the game, the more score you get, but the more enemies that spawn. You also only have five hits; once you lose your five lives, it's game over.

I didn't really have much time to develop this seamlessly, since I was incredibly sick when this project was assigned, but I still am proud of how the final game looks!
